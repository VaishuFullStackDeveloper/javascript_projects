About Our Village

Welcome to Shri Ganesh Gram Panchayat

Shri Ganesh Gram Panchayat is a vibrant village located in the heart of Maharashtra. With a legacy of unity, agriculture, and development, we are committed to making our village smart, sustainable, and self-reliant.

  • Strong community participation
  • Award-winning water conservation programs
  • 100% digital birth/death certificate system
  • Fully connected village with Wi-Fi

Our Mission

To provide transparent governance, quality rural infrastructure, and empower every villager digitally and socially.

Our Vision

To be a model digital village recognized for innovation, inclusiveness, and sustainability across India.
