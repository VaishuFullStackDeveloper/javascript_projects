Our History

Shri Ganesh Gram Panchayat was established in 1975 with the vision of promoting rural development and ensuring transparent governance. Over the past 50 years, we have transformed from a small rural settlement to a model panchayat, setting examples in rural development, education, and community welfare.

Our Vision

To create a self-reliant, prosperous, and sustainable rural community where every citizen has access to basic amenities, employment opportunities, and quality education while preserving our cultural heritage.

Our Objectives

  • Ensure 100% coverage of basic amenities like water, electricity, and sanitation
  • Promote sustainable agriculture and rural employment
  • Maintain transparency in governance and decision-making
  • Foster education and skill development programs
  • Preserve and promote local culture and traditions
